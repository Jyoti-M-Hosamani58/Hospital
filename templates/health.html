General medicine

Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating diseases of the heart, blood vessels, and circulatory system.

Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery is a medical specialty that involves diagnosing and treating diseases, disorders, and injuries to the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Nephro

Nephrology is a medical subspecialty that focuses on the kidneys and urinary tract, including their function and diseases.

Urology

Urology is a surgical specialty that focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the urinary tract and reproductive system.

Gastro

The gastrointestinal (GI) system, also known as the digestive tract or alimentary canal, is a passageway that leads from the mouth to the anus and includes the major organs of digestion.

General surgery

Paediatrics

Pediatrics is a medical specialty that focuses on the health of infants, children, adolescents, and young adults, from birth to about age 21.

Orthopedic

Orthopedics, also known as orthopedic surgery, is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of injuries and diseases that affect the musculoskeletal system.

Skin

Dermatosurgery means surgery of the damaged skin, which includes clinic based processing done using local anesthesia.

ENT

ENT stands for ear, nose, and throat, and ENT specialists, also known as otolaryngologists, are medical doctors and surgeons who treat conditions affecting these areas of the body.

Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology is the medical field that focuses on the health of the eye, including its anatomy, physiology, and diseases.
